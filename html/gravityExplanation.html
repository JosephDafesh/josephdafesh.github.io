Gravity is a game created to simulate a projectile being pulled on by various gravitational forces throughout space. The projectile's speed will be recalculated in real time, and the gravitational forces will be different depending on the randomly generated planet size and position.

See if you can find the point where all forces cancel each other out, or just have fun watching the cool projectiles hurdle everywhere. :)

Refreshing the game will remove all previous projectiles and randomly generate a new ecosystem.
